Hello!

To begin please select a degree you would like to view from the menu at the top left of the page.

You may view the currently selected course, department, and degree, as well as this help message from the tabs at the top of this pane. To change your current selections either click on the corresponding node in the tree view, or any of the corresponding links within the information pane.

Tree Pane

The tree pane displays a tree of courses required for the degree selected from the degree select dropdown, located at the top of the pane. Displayed courses are colour coded by virtue of dependencies; The warmer the colour, the more courses within the selected degree depend on it. Course tree nodes can be clicked to view additional information within the information pane.

Information Pane

Courses Tab

Within the course tab you will be able to find out everything about your selected course. You may also mark the course as taken by clicking the taken badge. By marking course you will be able to better see what courses you have taken at a glance.

Department Tab

The department tab displays all the information about the selected department. You cann access the department website and browse directly to the department's offered courses.

Degree Tab

The degree tab displays information of the currently selected degree.
